SolAR::datastructure::CamCalibration

A camera calibration matrix defining the intrinsic parameters of a camera.

Camera intrinsic parameters defined with a 3x3 matrix with the following format:

\[ \begin{matrix} f_x & 0 & c_x \\ 0 & f_y & c_y \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{matrix}\]

Where \(c_x\) and \(c_y\) define the optical center, \(f_x\) and \(f_y\) the focal.

SolAR::datastructure::CamDistortion

A camera distortion matrix defining the distortion parameters of a camera.

Camera distortion parameters defined with a vector with 5 floats with the following format: \( \left( K_1, K_2, P_1, P_2, K_3 \right) \)
Look at OpenCV documentation for more information.

SolAR::datastructure::PoseMatrix

A pose defining both position and orientation thanks to a 4x4 homogenous matrix.

A pose defined with a 4x4 matrix with the following format:

\[ \begin{matrix} R_{11} & R_{12} & R_{13} & T_x \\ R_{21} & R_{22} & R_{23} & T_y \\ R_{31} & R_{32} & R_{33} & T_z \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \end{matrix}\]

Where \(R\) is the 3x3 rotation matrix and \(T\) is the translation vector.
