What ?

The SolAR framework aims at providing you with a solution to build customized augmented reality services.

It will allow you easily developping your own camera pose estimation pipeline to develop robust and efficient AR services for your customers.

SolAR framework adapts to your specific needs

As your requirements are particular :

  • specific environment

  • device

  • accuracy

  • tracking area scale

you will find here all you need to build a customized and efficient solution to estimate your vision sensor pose for augmented reality purpose.
