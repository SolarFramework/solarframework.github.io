for whom is SolAR?

  • SolAR framework is for pose estimation experts, to make possible to create a pose estimation scheme with the best computer vision components.

  • SolAR framework is also for AR service developpers : they will benefit from the best pose estimation int their AR application.

SolAR adapts

As your requirements are particular :

  • specific environment

  • device

  • accuracy

  • tracking area scale

you will find here all you need to build a customized and efficient solution to estimate your vision sensor pose for augmented reality purpose.
