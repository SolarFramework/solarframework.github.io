SolAR, Solution for Augmented Reality

SolAR helps you to develop customized Augmented Reality solutions

SolAR is an open-source framework released under Apache license 2.0 making possible to easily create your own camera pose estimation solution to develop augmented reality applications.

By using SolAR, you can easily build augmented reality applications by testing different vision based localization solutions, create and share to the community your own camera pose estimation pipeline, and even develop efficient low-level processing elements to meet specific requirements.

SolAR is a framework improving interoperability for Augmented Reality applications that aims at offering efficiency and robustness thanks to a modular software pipeline architecture.

SolAR is always improving

SolAR is an open-souce project, join the community, contribute to the SolAR framework and participate to the augmented reality adoption.
